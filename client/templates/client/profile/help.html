Frequently Asked Questions and Privacy Policy

Here are some commonly asked questions and important information regarding your privacy and security when using our Blood Laboratory system:

What information is required for a blood test?

During a blood test, we collect personal and health information necessary to perform the test accurately.

How is my personal and health information protected?

We have stringent security measures in place to safeguard your personal and health information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Who can access my personal and health information?

Your personal and health information is accessible only to authorized personnel within our laboratory and may be shared with specific organizations under certain circumstances.

Can I access my test results?

You have the right to access your test results and can request them through the designated process.

How long is my personal and health information retained?

We retain your personal and health information for a specific duration and ensure its secure disposal once it is no longer required.

What happens in case of a data breach or unauthorized access?

We have established protocols to respond promptly to any data breaches or security incidents to mitigate potential risks.

Is my information shared with third parties?

Your information may be shared with healthcare providers, insurers, researchers, or other third parties, but only under specific circumstances.

How can I control the sharing of my information?

You have options to limit the use and disclosure of your information, such as opting out of research studies or setting restrictions on access to your records.

How can I create a strong and secure password?

To ensure password security, choose a combination of letters, numbers, and symbols to create a strong and difficult-to-guess password.

Can I enable two-factor authentication for added security?

You can enhance the security of your account by enabling two-factor authentication, requiring an additional verification factor along with your password.

What should I do if my account is compromised or hacked?

If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, take immediate action, such as changing your password, enabling two-factor authentication, or reporting the issue.

How can I avoid phishing scams and fake login pages?

Learn to recognize and avoid common phishing scams and fake login pages that attempt to deceive you into sharing your account information.

Can I remotely log out of my account or check active sessions?
